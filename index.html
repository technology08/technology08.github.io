Hello!

I am a rising senior from Cincinnati, Ohio at Stanford Online High School and Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy. I am deeply passionate about science, technology, medicine, and philosophy. In addition to being an Apple WWDC18 scholar, I have developed an app on the iOS App Store and have published a paper on Jupiter's synchrotron radiation in astrophysics. Further, I am the Education Co-Head at Innoverge International, where I develop new cutting-edge curriculum to expose and engage minority youth in interdisciplinary STEM workshops. In my free time, I enjoy pursuing neurological and philosophical research and conducting game nights with my friends. Moving forward, I am deeply excited to seek the convergence of biotechnology and philosophy, and help inspire others to follow their passions towards the pursuit of Truth as well.
